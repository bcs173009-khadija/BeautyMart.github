These Drugstore Makeup Products Are Perfect For Summer

With summer fast approaching, we’re dreaming of bronzed glam, dewy skin, and glossy lips. However, given the current climate, finding affordable options that’ll allow us to achieve our #SummerMakeupGoals is a must. From all-in-one face palettes fresh off the assembly line to bronzers that’ll sculpt all skin tones, here are five drugstore products for the ultimate summer glow up.

1. These Drugstore Makeup Products Are Perfect For Summer

Each Tropical Pop Kit features the Post Beach Glow Eye & Face palette and a juicy Lip Highlight in the shade Island Hopping. We’ll be honest, it was the all-in-one face palette that caught our attention. Not only does it contain three matte eyeshadows and five shimmers, but a stunning highlighter, as well as a sculpting shimmer bronzer that can double up as eyeshadow for a monochromatic makeup look. We also love the bold aqua shimmer shade, which perfectly contrasts with the golden summery hues for an eye-popping look.

2. The Ultimate Summer Lip:

Forget about matte lips this summer, it’s ALL about a sexy, juicy pout, and e.l.f. delivers exactly that with their new The Sheer Slick Lipstick, $5. The formula glides onto the lip with a sheer wash of color and a subtle, glossy finish, creating the illusion of a fuller lip. Available in six universally flattering shades from a tangy Grapefruit to a hot pink Jam or Jelly shade, there’s a shade for everyone. It’s also ideal for a no-makeup makeup look, which has become our go-to during lockdown.

3. For Sculpted Glam:

While the beauty industry is (finally) becoming more inclusive, the drugstore aisle still has some catching up to do. Makeup Revolution is making their mark their new Glow Splendor Bronzers, $12, which has garnered glowing reviews thanks to their inclusive, wide-ranging shade collection.

4. The 2020 Essential:

2020 is the year of blusher, and the e.l.f Jelly Pop Flush Blush, $6, is a great affordable option that comes in two universally flattering colors. The easy-to-build blush has a jelly-like texture that blends seamlessly into the skin for a healthy flush, or you can layer up the color for a more dramatic look. The formula is also infused with hydrating ingredients like watermelon, argon oil, vitamin E, and castor oil to give your skin a radiant, dewy finish – hello summer! It also smells divine!
